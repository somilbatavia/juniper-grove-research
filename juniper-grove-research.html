Our Philosophy

In a market full of noise,
we chose silence.

Juniper Grove Research was founded by two brothers who believe the best investment ideas come from deep, patient study — not from chasing headlines or following the crowd.

Each week, we enter the grove. We study one company with the discipline of scholars and the conviction of allocators. When we emerge, we share what we've found — a single investment thesis, rigorously built and clearly presented.

No filler. No fluff. No fifteen stocks to watch this week. Just one name, one thesis, and the complete research to back it up.

"The stock market is a device for transferring money from the impatient to the patient."

Warren Buffett

Our Approach

Three pillars of conviction

Concentration

One stock per week. We believe depth defeats breadth. While others scan hundreds of tickers, we study one until we understand it completely.

Transparency

Every thesis comes with the models, assumptions, and research behind it. We don't just tell you what we think — we show you how we got there.

Independence

No sponsors. No conflicts. No one paying us to say something we don't believe. Our only obligation is to the quality of our research.

The juniper endures where other trees cannot. It grows slowly, roots deeply, and outlasts everything around it. That is how we invest.
